My thoughts keep getting worse

September 30, 2020 by Donad

It has been one day since I last updated you on my random thoughts. Today’s blog wont be as random, but just me talking to you about the process of getting this blog up and running. It started with my lab document outlining all the components that I would need to create my webpage for this blog. It was simple enough in the beginning but like everything in life it grew increasingly difficult. This led me to watch countless hours of youtube which only helped me to spin faster in circles. It wasn’t until I got assistance that I figured out what was expected of me.

Even though the instructions were clearly stated in the lab, there were some omissions of vital information that played with my sanity. And to be completely honest I would not have been able to complete the lab without assistance. But here we are with a finished product that hopefully meets all criteria.
